# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in this project to be a welcoming, respectful, and
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.
We pledge to act in ways that keep the community open and friendly.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that helps the community:

- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users.
- Showing empathy towards other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour include personal attacks, insulting or
derogatory comments, harassment of any kind, and publishing others' private
information without permission.

## Adoption

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1, as the full text of its code of conduct. Where this summary and the
Contributor Covenant differ, the Contributor Covenant governs.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, report it privately to the
maintainers through GitHub's private vulnerability reporting (the repository's
**Security** tab → **Report a vulnerability**). Reports are handled
confidentially and reviewed as promptly as the maintainers are able.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with
this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently restrict any
contributor for behaviour they deem inappropriate.
